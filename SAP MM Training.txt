Life cycle of a purchase order:
	1) Purchase order : First Purchare is created by the business to the vendor
	2) Goods receipt (GR) : Receiving the goods into the ware house
	3) Invoice receipt (IR) : Then invoice is generated ang given to the business by the venor
	4) Payment : Finally payment is made to the vendor.
